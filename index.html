Introduction

As a Fellow Chartered Accountant and a certified forensic auditor, I bring over a decade of diverse professional experience, including a rewarding tenure with Deloitte in its Audit & Assurance Department.

Currently, I serve as the Senior Partner heading the central region at M/s M. Borar & Co., Chartered Accountants, a distinguished firm established in 1990. My expertise lies in the fields of Audit and Assurance, complemented by proficiency in offering sector-agnostic consultancy services tailored to client needs.

In addition to my professional journey, I have been actively involved in the growth of the Indore Branch of ICAI. I have had the privilege of serving as the CICASA Chairman, Treasurer, and Secretary of the Indore Branch of ICAI, contributing significantly to its initiatives and success

On the field, I am the Vice-Captain of the Indore Branch CIRC of the ICAI cricket team and an all-round athlete, excelling in sports like football, badminton, sprinting, and chess. My passion for sports reflects my commitment to teamwork, strategy, and perseverance.

Recognized for my dynamic personality, I am often described as humble, approachable, and driven by a zeal for innovation and adaptability. My ability to present ideas with clarity and confidence has made me a sought-after speaker at ICAI forums, business associations, and B-schools.

I am now seeking the opportunity to again serve the ICAI Indore Branch with a steadfast commitment to the growth of our profession and the welfare of its members. With your support, I aim to bring impactful initiatives and meaningful contributions to the branch's legacy.

Manifesto

With your support, I aim to prioritize the following areas to strengthen and elevate our profession, if elected

  • Enhancing the Brand Image of the Indore Branch

    Establishing a dedicated column in leading newspapers to publish knowledgeable and insightful articles, showcasing the expertise and contributions of our members.

  • Informative Newsletters

    Publishing high-quality newsletters featuring significant judgments of cases fought by members of the Indore Branch, highlighting their achievements and legal insights.

  • New Libraries and Reading Rooms

    Setting up additional libraries and reading rooms across Indore to provide a conducive learning environment for CA students.

  • Comprehensive Information Repository for Students

    Creating a centralized repository of all essential information on accommodation, food, and other necessities for CA students coming to Indore.

  • Travel Assistance for Outstation Students

    Organizing travel assistance for students attending training programs by the Institute, ensuring a hassle-free experience.

  • Enforcement and Support Committee for Chartered Accountants

    Establishing a dedicated committee of influential and experienced Chartered Accountants to provide support and assistance to members facing unwarranted actions by police or other law enforcement authorities. This committee will act as a safeguard, ensuring that CAs are not made easy targets and receive the necessary guidance and representation in such situations.

  • Tie-ups and Support for Members

    Establishing more partnerships with hotels, hospitals, and other service providers to avail facilities at subsidized rates for members.

  • E-Library for Members

    Developing an e-library accessible to all members, containing videos of seminars, records of disciplinary cases, and a repository of recent case laws and judgments for instant access.

Your Vision, My Mission

I believe the strength of the Indore Branch lies in the collective wisdom and ideas of its members. As part of my commitment to inclusivity and growth, I invite you to share your suggestions and initiatives for what the Indore Branch should focus on in the future.

If elected, I pledge to carefully evaluate and strive to implement these ideas to enhance the branch's services and bring value to its members. Let’s work together to shape a brighter and more impactful future for the Indore Branch of ICAI.
